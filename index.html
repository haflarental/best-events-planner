Got Questions?

Answers to the Most Common Questions

If you don't find what you're looking for, just send us a message on WhatsApp.

Are there any VAT charges or hidden fees? +
No, we do not charge VAT, as our turnover is below the tax registration limit, and there are no hidden charges. All costs are shared clearly before booking confirmation.
Does "Hafla Rental" require an advance deposit to confirm booking? +
Yes, a booking is not confirmed until a 50% advance deposit is received. The remaining balance must be paid on the day of the event.
What is your cancellation policy? +
Our cancellation policy depends on how close the cancellation is to the event date and the services booked.

1. Cancellation 7 or More Days Prior: 90% to 100% refund
2. Cancellation 2 to 6 Days Prior: 80% to 90% refund
3. Cancellation 1 Day Before: 60% to 80% refund
4. Cancellation on Event Date: 0% to 60% refund

The exact refund percentage depends on the type of services booked and any costs already incurred.
What should I do if I receive a damaged or non-working product? +
We ensure all products are delivered in proper working condition. However, if you receive a damaged product or if any item stops working during the event, please inform us immediately on WhatsApp with details. We will arrange a replacement or suitable solution, depending on the issue and availability.
